John Prine: The Songwriter GOAT

John's final album, The Tree Of Forgiveness, was the crowning glory of one of the most remarkable musical careers of all time. He may not be the world's most famous songwriter, but ask your favorite songwriter who their favorite songwriter was, and, more often than not, they will respond "John Prine!"

Some of his most beloved hits include:

  • Lake Marie
  • Sam Stone
  • Illegal Smile
  • Summer's End
